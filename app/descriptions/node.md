Node App is a technology company that provides an **influencer marketing and content generation platform**. Their software helps brands connect with a community of verified micro-influencers to promote products and generate user-generated content (UGC) for social media. 

## Key Functions

The platform offers several services to help brands manage their social media campaigns: 
- **Influencer Outreach**: Brands can easily find and connect with relevant influencers (including foodies, lifestyle, beauty gurus, etc.) to partner with them on marketing campaigns.
- **Campaign Management**: The platform streamlines the process of launching campaigns, from setting content briefs and target demographics to tracking progress and influencer reach.
- **Content Generation**: Influencers create organic photo and video content featuring the brand's products, which the brand can then reuse for its own marketing efforts.
- **Local Targeting**: The service allows businesses, particularly in industries like food & beverage and hospitality, to target local influencers within a specific radius to drive local clientele and boost in-person visits.
- Analytics and Tracking: Node App provides tools to track the performance and reach of campaigns and content produced by the influencers. 

---

In essence, Node App serves as a one-stop solution for brands looking to leverage influencer marketing efficiently to increase brand visibility and generate authentic content. Talent management agency **Dulcedo Group** officially acquired the AI-powered influencer marketing platform Node App in September 2024 in a cash-and-equity deal that valued Node at approximately **$13 million**.
